Meet Your Personal Trainer - John Johnson

Hi! I'm John Johnson, certified personal trainer, nutrition coach, and founder of FitCore Personal Training. For over eight years, I've helped more than 200 busy professionals and parents transform their health, gain confidence, and achieve their dream bodies—without crash diets or endless hours at the gym.
